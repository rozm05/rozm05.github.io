My Favorite Characters in Genshin Impact

Nahida

Raiden Shogun Furina Yae Miko

Nahida is a playable character in Genshin Impact, also known as Lesser Lord Kusanali. She is the Dendro Archon of Sumeru and god of Wisdom.

Abilities

Nahida's elemental skill, All Schemes to Know, binds her enemies and marks them with the Seed of Skandha. Triggering elemental reactions on a marked enemy unleashes Tri-Karma Purification on all marked enemies.

When using her ultimate ability, Illusory Heart, Nahida spawns the Shrine of Maya. The Shrine buffs all allies based on the Elemental Types present within the party.
